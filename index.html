Objective-driven Video Ad Generation for Brands using Deep Learning Techniques
Research Intern at Adobe Systems, 2017

My work served as a proof of concept to leverage the vast amount of unused data using weak supervision. Specifically, I worked to utilize social media videos to create promotional videos for brands. I built an RNN-based autoregressive model that would output a sequence of features based on a training corpus of brand videos. Using these features, I extracted relevant video segments from a pool of social media videos via clustering techniques and used those segments to stitch a new video.
