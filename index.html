Objective-driven Video Ad Generation for Brands using Deep Learning Techniques
Research Intern at Adobe Systems, 2017

The work served as a proof of concept to leverage the vast amount of unused data using weak supervision. Specifically, our goal was to utilize social media videos to create promotional videos for brands. We built an RNN-based model that would output a sequence of features based on a training corpus of brand videos. Using these features, relevant video segments can be extracted from a pool of social media videos via clustering techniques to stitch together a new video.
